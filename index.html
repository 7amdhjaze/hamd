Pizza

I love pizza because it is one of the most delicious foods. It has a
soft crust, tasty cheese, and many different
toppings like vegetables or meat. Pizza is also easy to share with family and friends,
which makes it more enjoyable. That is why pizza is my favorite food

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the dough
  3. Add cheese on top of the sauce
  4. Put vegetables or meat as toppings
  5. Bake the pizza in the oven
  6. Cut and serve hot
